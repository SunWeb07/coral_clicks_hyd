Our Story

Where Every Frame Tells a Story

Founded by Sohil Dhanani, Coral Clicks was born from a deep passion for storytelling through the lens. We believe that every smile, every tear, every fleeting glance holds a universe of emotion — and it's our mission to preserve those universes forever.

Based in Hyderabad, we specialize in crafting cinematic visual narratives for weddings, celebrations, and brand stories. With an eye for authentic moments and a commitment to artistic excellence, we've had the privilege of documenting hundreds of life's most precious chapters.
